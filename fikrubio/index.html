Projects

Pacman

“Pacman” entertains viewers. He is laughing loud while travelling along the screen. It is the magic of computer that assimilate pacman as if he opens and closes his mouth. Interestingly, JavaScript swaps image icons behind the scene. which were stored on the file, at certain time and position. Browser, executes HTML, CSS and JavaScript files to perform things that we see on screen. Computer is magic machine that controls light speeds and color it to us so that our eye able to see it. Amazing invention. Click "SHOW PACMAN" to see what he is doing.

Bus Stop Tracker

This is the second interesting project in my portfolio. The “Bus Stop Tracker” tracks locations on real time. It picks latitude and longitude data and marks those points on the screen. In such manner it tracks the position of moving object such cars, ships, plans, bullets, missiles, etc. Thanks to technology, GPS has made our life easier when driving or walking in big cities like New Your or London. Life eased because of technics like the one we used in this course. Click "SHOW BUS STOP TRACKER" to see the map; where bus tracker moves when you click button at top right corner. To repeat markert movement, pls refresh your browser.Pls visit links on header menu to visit other interesting projects created while attending full stack developer training.

Painting

Paintings are exhibit that shows colorful balls “raining” to create a paint on the screen. Every fraction of a second, balls of color popup on the screen at random location. Behind the scene, JavaScript randomly picks a point and styles that in the form of ball shape and color. Click "SHOW PAINTINGS" to view the paint. Close your screen once you see enough of the balls printed to the screen.
