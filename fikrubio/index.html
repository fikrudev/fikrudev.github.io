Pacman

“Pacman” entertains viewers. He is laughing loud while travelling along the screen. In fact he is not. It is computer that assimlate as if pacman is laughing. Interestingly, it is JavaScript that swaps image icons, which were stored on the file, at certain time and position. Browser, executes codes written on HTML, CSS and Java Script to perform things that looks magic. In fact, computer is magic machine that delays/simulating light, that is travelling at its speed (3x10^8m/s). Amazing human invention. Click PACMAN SHOW to see what he is doing.

Bus Stop Tracker

This is the second interesting project in my portfolio. The “Bus Stop Tracker” tracks locations on real time. It picks latitude and longitude data and marks those points on the screen. In such manner it tracks the position of moving object such cars, ships, plans, bullets, missiles, etc. Thanks to technology, GPS has made our life easier when driving or walking in big cities like New Your or London. Life eased because of technics like the one we used in this course. Click BUS STOP TRACKER to see the map; where bus tracker moves when you click button at top right corner.
